About NESH Group

Transforming Ethiopia's agricultural heritage into global premium food excellence

Our Mission

To create Ethiopia's first vertically-integrated premium food ecosystem that delivers operational excellence from Ethiopian highlands to global tables through sustainable, traceable, and innovative practices.

Our Vision

To become Africa's premier food group, recognized globally for transforming Ethiopian agricultural heritage into premium products that set new standards in quality, sustainability, and taste.

Our Values

Excellence in every detail, sustainability in every process, heritage in every product, and integrity in every partnership.

Our Story

From vision to reality: Building Ethiopia's premium food legacy

Founded in 2023, NESH Group emerged from a simple yet powerful observation: Ethiopia possesses some of the world's most unique agricultural treasures, yet lacks a premium integrated food system to deliver these treasures to global markets with the excellence they deserve.

Our name, NESH, embodies our commitment: Nourishment, Excellence, Sustainability, Heritage.

Today, we operate three vertically-integrated enterprises: Nesh Artisan Bakery, Nesh Premium Dairy, and Nesh Teff Heritage.

Our Sustainability Commitment

Building a food ecosystem that nourishes people and planet

Regenerative Agriculture

Partnering with 500+ smallholder farmers using sustainable farming practices that improve soil health and biodiversity.
